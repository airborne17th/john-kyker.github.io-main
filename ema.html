Welcome to Shrine Board Wishes

Konnichiwa (こんにちは)! Ema (絵馬) are small wooden plaques that are common to Japan. These are used to write prayers or wishes. Ema are left hanging up at the shrine, where the kami (神) are believed to receive them. Annually this is done during New Years. Post your New Years resolution here and view other plaques. If you don't put a name it will be left anonymous.
